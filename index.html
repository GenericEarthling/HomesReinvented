About

At Homes Reinvented, we enjoy designing and working with clients on projects that are both large and small. Helping clients find their style and pulling together a cohesive space is our goal.  We work with you to create a home that is comfortable, inviting and in your own style.  With home owners as well as investors it is our sincere goal to maximize your investment potential, complete the project on time, and finish on budget!

The Homes Reinvented Process

  • First meeting we walk through your home or investment property with you for an initial brain-storming session to discuss possible room arrangement and or change floor plans. Discover your style and figure out your goals for the space. Together we come up with a plan to meet your timeline and budget.
  • Second meeting we will provide ideas, basic plans (if required) and finish selections. Clients will receive 2 or more complete suggestions.
  • Once you decide on a direction. We can use your trade contacts or ours. Either way, we work for and with you to create a beautifully designed cohesive home.

Our number one goal at Homes Reinvented is a fresh new “Reinvented Home” you will be proud of for years to come.
