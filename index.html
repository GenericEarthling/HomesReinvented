About

At Homes Reinvented, we enjoy designing and working with clients whether it’s a large apartment building or a small closet. Helping clients find their style and pulling together a cohesive space is very rewarding. Our goal is to leave you with a home that is comfortable, inviting and in your own style. We help you with Professional finish selections for your home or investment property. It is our sincere goal to maximize your investment potential, complete the project on time, and finish on budget! In addition, we enjoy providing the eventual new home owner with a fresh new “Reinvented Home” they will be proud of for years to come.

Beverly Belanger Marquart

Senior Designer and CEO

The Homes Reinvented Process

  • We will walk through your home or investment property for an initial brain-storming session to discuss possible room arrangement and wall removals
  • We come up with a plan for your timeline and budget
  • We will provide ideas and basic plans with all finish selections. Client will receive 1-2 complete suggestions of finishes to choose from
  • We make every attempt to find the least expensive, yet most appealing and up to date finishes
  • Extensive research has and will go into all selections
  • We shop for the client or with the client
  • We schedule all trades and see work to completion

We can use your trade contacts or ours. Either way, we leave you in a beautifully designed cohesive home.
